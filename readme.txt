== Add Subscription Forms to WordPress. Send transactional Emails and Automate your email marketing efforts.  ==

MailRush.io Forms makes easy the implementation of Newsletter subscriptions in your WordPress website.

= MailRush.io Features =
* **Mailing List Subscriptions:** Add Subscriptions form using our Widget. Add it to the sidebar, footer or even within content.
* **Send Transactional Emails from WordPress:** By activating the transactional Email feature, all your WordPress Emails will be sent via MailRush.io
* **Campaign Managment:** Start and Stop campaigns as needed. MailRush.io will automate the delivery.
* **Delivery Stats:** Track delivery rate, open rate, bound rate, blocked rate of your Email Marketing Campaigns.
* **Contact Filtering:** Segmentate your contact list with our robust contact list filtering.
* **Contact List Management:** Import, Segmentate, Export your contact list without limitations.
* **Spam Score Check:** Automate Email Marketing Best Practice before starting your Emailing.
* **Cold Email Marketing Support:** Engage new prospect using the outreach tool.

For a full list of features, please visit [MailRush.io Email Marketing Tool](https://mailrush.io).

https://www.youtube.com/embed/DEvj_VusjCM

== Installation ==

* Install and activate the Wordpress Plugin
* After installation, under Settings, click the MailRush.io option
* Create a new MailRush.io account, or enter your API Key.
